# roket mods

## engine mods
- move_speed_mod - multiplier of movement speed
- boost_upgrade - enables temporary boosting

## weapon mods
- laser_upgrade - enables usage of old legacy laser

## hull mods
- hull_strength_mod - adds lives to the ship
- shield_upgrade - enables shield buff spawning and collecting

## ability mods
- ghost_upgrade - enables usage of old runner ghosting
- heal_drone_upgrade - enables summoning of old tank delivery drone
